Biography

I am a second year PhD student at the Alan Turing Institute and the University of Warwick supervised by Theodoros Damoulas. I am part of the London air quality project at the Turing and the Warwick Machine Learning Group. My research interests lie in scalable inference methods for spatio-temporal Bayesian methods such as Gaussian Processes.
